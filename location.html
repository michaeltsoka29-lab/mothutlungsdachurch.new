Service Times

Divine Worship Service

Mid-Week Prayer

Common Questions

What should I wear?

You'll see everything from suits to jeans. Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable and respectful.

What about my children?

We provide safe, fun, and age-appropriate Bible classes for children during the 9:30 AM hour.

Is there a lunch?

Yes! Most Sabbaths we share a vegetarian "Potluck" meal after the 12:00 PM service. You are our honored guest!
